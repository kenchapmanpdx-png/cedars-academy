Meet the Founder

Built by an Artist, For Artists

With over 12 years in the beauty industry, Cedar has worked with pageant queens, bridal parties, editorial teams, and everyday women who just wanted to feel amazing. She started Cedars Academy because she saw too many talented people held back by access — access to training, to mentorship, to someone who actually cared about their success.

Today, the academy is a community. Students become family. Graduates come back to mentor the next class. That's not a marketing line — that's just how it works here.
