Meet the Founder & CEO

More Than Just Beauty

I believe makeup is more than just beauty — it’s art, empowerment, and opportunity. Over the years, I’ve dedicated my career to creating a space where aspiring makeup artists can learn, grow, and transform their passion into a profession.

What excites me most is seeing students take that first step into their journey — because I know how life-changing it can be.

At our academy, you’ll not only learn the techniques that make artistry powerful, but you’ll also gain the confidence, branding skills, and business knowledge to stand out in this industry.

If you’re here, it means you’re ready — ready to invest in yourself, ready to pursue your passion, and ready to create something bigger than you imagined. I’m thrilled to walk this path with you.
